üîß Common Fixes

AI Being Too Verbose?

Add: "Be concise. Maximum 3 paragraphs."

AI Being Too Cautious?

Add: "I'm a professional. Give me the direct answer."

Output Not Right Format?

Add: "Output ONLY the [JSON/code/etc]. No explanation."

AI Hallucinating?

Add: "If you're not certain, say so. Don't make things up."
