Getting Started with AI Assistants

The Big Three: Claude, ChatGPT, and Microsoft Copilot

Claude (by Anthropic)

Best for: Long-form writing, nuanced analysis, document review, coding assistance

üí° Pro Tip

Claude excels when you give it a role and context. Instead of "write an email," try: "You're a senior project manager at a software development firm. Write a professional email to a client explaining a two-week delay due to scope changes, maintaining a positive relationship while being direct about the situation."

  • Key strengths: Exceptional at following complex instructions, strong reasoning, 200K token context window
  • Pricing: Free tier available | Pro: $20/month | Team: $25/user/month | Enterprise: Custom

ChatGPT (by OpenAI)

Best for: General-purpose assistance, creative tasks, quick answers, image generation

  • Key strengths: Largest ecosystem, DALL-E image generation, internet browsing, voice mode
  • Pricing: Free tier (GPT-3.5) | Plus: $20/month | Team: $25-30/user/month | Enterprise: Custom

Microsoft Copilot

Best for: Integration with Microsoft 365, enterprise environments using Microsoft stack

  • Key strengths: Deep integration with Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams
  • Pricing: Free tier | Copilot Pro: $20/month | Microsoft 365 Copilot: $30/user/month

üìã Recommended Setup Approach

  1. Start with one tool ‚Äî Pick Claude or ChatGPT and use it consistently for 2 weeks
  2. Add Microsoft Copilot if your firm uses Microsoft 365 heavily
  3. Don't try to learn all three simultaneously ‚Äî You'll develop intuition for when to use each

Daily Task Acceleration

The 5 High-Impact Use Cases

1. Email Drafting

The old way: Stare at blank screen, write, rewrite, second-guess tone.

The AI way: Provide context, key points, desired tone, and let AI generate a first draft in seconds.

Email Type Time Before AI Time With AI Savings
Client status update 15-20 min 3-5 min 75%
Proposal follow-up 20-30 min 5-8 min 70%
Difficult conversation 30-45 min 10-15 min 65%

2. Meeting Summaries

AI can transform rough meeting notes into structured summaries with action items in seconds.

3. Brainstorming & Ideation

Use AI as a thought partner to generate options, challenge assumptions, and explore angles you might miss.

4. Research & Synthesis

AI excels at synthesizing information from multiple sources into coherent summaries.

5. First Drafts of Any Document

Proposals, reports, memos, presentations ‚Äî AI can generate solid first drafts that you refine.

Document Review and Synthesis

The Document Review Framework

AI dramatically accelerates document review, but the approach matters:

  1. Upload/paste the document ‚Äî Most AI tools handle up to 100+ pages
  2. Start with summarization ‚Äî "Summarize this document in 5 bullet points"
  3. Drill into specifics ‚Äî Ask targeted questions about sections
  4. Extract structured data ‚Äî "List all obligations, deadlines, and dollar amounts"
  5. Generate outputs ‚Äî Create response documents, comparison tables, action items

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Caution

Never share confidential client documents with consumer AI tools unless you have explicit permission and understand the data handling policies. Use enterprise versions for sensitive content.

Building the AI Habit

Identifying Opportunities in Your Daily Workflow

The executives who gain the most from AI aren't necessarily the most technical ‚Äî they're the ones who build the habit of asking "Could AI help with this?"

The AI Opportunity Checklist

Ask yourself before each task:

  • Am I starting from a blank page? ‚Üí AI can generate a first draft
  • Am I reviewing or synthesizing information? ‚Üí AI can summarize and extract
  • Am I writing something I've written before? ‚Üí AI can adapt templates
  • Am I trying to communicate something complex? ‚Üí AI can help structure and clarify
  • Am I stuck or procrastinating? ‚Üí AI can help you start

üèãÔ∏è Hands-On Exercise: Complete a Real Task

Duration: 15-20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Open your actual email inbox or task list
  2. Identify one real task you need to complete today:
    • An email you need to write
    • A document you need to review
    • Notes you need to summarize

  3. Open Claude (claude.ai) or ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)

  5. Note how long it took and the quality of the output

Reflection Questions:

  • How much time did you save compared to doing this manually?
  • What surprised you about the AI's output?
  • What would you do differently next time?

Key Takeaways

  1. Start with one tool ‚Äî Claude or ChatGPT, used consistently, beats trying to learn everything at once
  2. AI excels at first drafts ‚Äî Let AI generate, then you refine and personalize
  3. Context is everything ‚Äî Better prompts with role, context, and constraints produce dramatically better outputs
  4. Build the habit ‚Äî Before each task, ask "Could AI help with this?"
  5. Time savings compound ‚Äî Small efficiencies across dozens of daily tasks add up to hours saved
