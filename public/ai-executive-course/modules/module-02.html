AI in Excel - Financial Modeling & Analysis

The New Excel Workflow

Traditional approach: Build formulas manually, copy patterns, debug errors, create charts one by one.

AI-enhanced approach: Describe what you want in natural language, let AI generate formulas and analysis, refine and verify.

Microsoft 365 Copilot in Excel

What it can do:

  • Analyze data and provide insights ‚Äî "What are the trends in this data?" "Which projects were most profitable last quarter?"
  • Create formulas from descriptions ‚Äî "Calculate the running total of revenue by month"
  • Generate visualizations ‚Äî "Create a chart showing revenue by client over time"
  • Build models ‚Äî "Create a 12-month cash flow projection based on this historical data"

üí° When You Don't Have Copilot

Use Claude or ChatGPT with copy/paste! Describe your spreadsheet structure, paste your data, and ask for formulas or analysis. You can then copy the formulas back into Excel.

Practical Financial Analysis Tasks

Cash Flow Forecasting

Ask AI to create a 12-month cash flow forecast template including revenue recognition, recurring revenue, payroll, variable costs, fixed costs, and tax payments‚Äîwith baseline, optimistic, and pessimistic scenarios.

Utilization Analysis

AI can help calculate individual utilization rates, department averages, trend analysis, and flag anyone consistently under or over utilized.

Project Profitability

Analyze gross margin by project, net margin after overhead, comparison to estimates, and patterns across project types.

Contract and Proposal Review

AI-Assisted Contract Review

Use case: Quickly understand key terms, identify risks, and prepare questions.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Caveat

AI is not legal advice. Always have contracts reviewed by qualified legal counsel for significant deals.

Effective Contract Review Prompt

Ask AI to provide:

  1. Plain English Summary ‚Äî What you're agreeing to, key obligations
  2. Key Commercial Terms ‚Äî Payment, renewal, pricing escalation
  3. Risk Analysis ‚Äî Liability, indemnification, IP ownership, termination
  4. Red Flags ‚Äî Unusual or one-sided terms
  5. Questions to Ask ‚Äî Clarifications and negotiation points

RFP Response Acceleration

AI can break down an RFP into a response checklist, identifying each requirement, where to address it, and whether you have a strong, moderate, or weak position.

Process Documentation and SOPs

Why This Matters

  • Scalability: Documented processes allow you to grow without chaos
  • Consistency: Same quality regardless of who does the work
  • Training: New team members get up to speed faster
  • Compliance: Audit trails and documented procedures

AI-Assisted SOP Creation

Turn tribal knowledge into formal documentation by telling AI:

  • Who does the process and when it happens
  • Rough list of steps involved
  • Tools and systems used
  • Common issues that occur

AI can then create a detailed SOP including purpose, roles, step-by-step procedures, quality checkpoints, and troubleshooting guides.
